James Baker's internship overview.

For the summers of 2019, 2020, and 2021, I worked at Google where I covered a wide gamut of core Google technologies as well as some cutting edge research areas.

1. In 2019, I worked in Google's "top-secret moonshot factory" where I helped develop a web-scale cybersecurity tool designed for indexing, aliasing, and tracking the world's telemetry data.

2. In 2020, I worked on Google's Retail Search product aimed at bringing Google quality search natively to retailers websites. I was awarded a Guaranteed Return Offer (offered to top interns for exceptional achievements during internship) for my work.

3. In 2021, I worked as more of a researcher for Google's Search team investigating advanced natural language query quality heuristics.


My work at Google is protected under a non-disclosure agreement therefore I do not have artifacts I can share. However, I do have some photos/videos I took during my time there.
