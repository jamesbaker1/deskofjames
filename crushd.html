Crush'd

James Baker's crush'd overview.

Beginning in March 2020, I co-founded a university-specific dating application that amassed over 5000 users and was written about in multiple news outlets. The premise of the web application was that only University of Michigan students could anonymously and digitally crush another classmate. The crush recipient would then receive an email that someone at school crush'd them and in order to reveal the sender's identity, the recipient would have to crush them back.

In Fall 2021, I plan on expanding the application to over 30 other campuses. Wish me luck!
